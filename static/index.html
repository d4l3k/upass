Note on Security

In general, entering your password into an unknown site is a terrible idea. If you enter your password I can't guarantee the safety of your password but I have taken several steps to ensure it's security. The connection between your computer is encrypted with SSL and your password is encrypted with 4096-bit RSA encryption. The decryption key is not saved on the server and must be manually loaded entered time it launches. Thus, if the server is ever compromised, there's little risk that they will get anything useful. If you're worried, I encourage you to launch your own copy of this server since it's open source.
